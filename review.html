A mixed salad is a refreshing and nutritious dish made by combining a variety of fresh vegetables, and sometimes fruits, to create a colorful and flavorful meal. It usually includes ingredients like lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, onions, and carrots, but can also be enhanced with items such as bell peppers, sweet corn,

radishes, or olives. To add more taste and texture, people often include cheese, croutons, or nuts, along with a light dressing such as olive oil, lemon juice, or vinaigrette. Mixed salads are not only healthy and easy to prepare but also versatile, as they can be served as a side dish or enjoyed as a complete meal.
